# BARCODE (Hong Kong) - Cocktail Bar Partner Notes

---

## 🧭 Snapshot

**BARCODE** is a Central cocktail cafe-bar with a “day coffee / night cocktails” concept, and an after-hours vibe that’s described as a **sleek, hidden speakeasy** experience.

*Quick positioning:* a coffee + dessert culture concept that transitions into ingredient-forward, story-driven cocktails at night.

Sources:
- Time Out (relocation + concept): https://www.timeout.com/hong-kong/bars-and-pubs/barcode
- Foodie (signature cocktails / “4 elements of matter” theme): https://www.afoodieworld.com/blog/2022/10/11/barcode-s-new-menu/
- Sippit (listing + location + hours): https://sippit.app/places/ChIJrzW3uTkBBDQR6DvagOwcL8Q

## 📍 Location (Central, Hong Kong)

**Address:** `UF/F, 45 Cochrane St, Central, Hong Kong`

Time Out notes BARCODE **relocated** from its previous location on **Glenealy Street** to a **new Cochrane Street location underneath the Mid-Levels Escalator**, integrating cafe + bar areas into one space.

## 🎨 Brand / Interior Cues

Time Out highlights:
- **White base** with **blue accents** (sofas)
- **Sheer cream-coloured curtains**
- **Large windows** with **natural sunlight**
- New venue removes the earlier small porch compared to the old setup

The overall guest feel is “bright + relaxed during day”, turning into “sleek + story-forward cocktails after hours”.

## 🏷️ Tagline & Philosophy (BARCODE kiosk copy)

Tagline: **WHERE EVERY DRINK TELLS A STORY**

Brand story framing (from the BARCODE kiosk UI text in this repo):
- Every cocktail is presented as an **experience**, not just ingredients in a glass.
- Their **smart table / interactive coasters** detect and identify the cocktail and trigger **visual animations** that complement the drink’s character.

## 🥂 Signature Cocktails (4 Elements of Matter theme)

Foodie describes a menu refresh (first anniversary) featuring **signature cocktails inspired by the four elements of matter**, priced at **~HK$130**.

| Element Theme | Cocktail | Visual / Flavor Hook (for creative direction) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Air | **Beyond the Clouds** | Bright **pink** with a mound of **candy floss**; strawberry notes; **croissant-infused vodka** for a creamy finish |
| Earth | **Man Go** | **Green mango-infused** grappa + pisco/tonic/pineapple; **glows bioluminescent blue** (quinine); tart fruity ending |
| Ocean / Water | **Under the Sea** | Spicy, salty **Bloody Mary**; served in a **ceramic oyster vessel**; oyster vodka + tomato/basil brine + lemon + Tabasco/Worcestershire |
| Earth (2nd) | **Gin Zu** | Pear-infused London dry gin + dry vermouth + yuzu; **wasabi + ginger** for a kick; **sesame oil** finishing aroma |
| Fire | **Rubi Cube** | Applewood rum + rhubarb + red apple + Angostura bitters; **smoky + sweet**, then subtly bitter |

## 🤝 Collaboration Hooks for Tales9 (why BARCODE fits)
