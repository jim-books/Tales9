   ## Product Overview

   This is a **33.2-inch 1:1 square display monitor** with a **1900 × 1900** resolution, designed for indoor information display applications. The unit runs on **Android 8.1** and is powered by a **quad-core processor**, supporting playback of various video, audio, and image formats. It also supports **Ethernet** and **Wi-Fi** connectivity, with optional **4G/5G** network modules.

   The enclosure features a **sheet metal bezel** and **cold-rolled steel housing**, with the front protected by **≥3.5 mm high-strength explosion-proof tempered glass**. It supports both **wall-mounted** and **suspended** installation. The display is energy-efficient, with standby power consumption of no more than **1 W** and operating power consumption of no more than **65 W**.
